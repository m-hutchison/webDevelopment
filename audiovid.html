Multimedia Audio and Video



Things that I have learnt about audio and video in Multimedia:

What I learnt about analog and digital television standards
I learnt that there are three analog standards (NTSC, PAL and SECAM)

What I learnt about National Television System Committee (NTSC)
I learnt that NTSC uses 525 lines per screen at 30 frames per second

What I learnt about Phase Alternating Line (PAL)
I learnt that PAL uses 625 lines per screen at 25 frames per second

What I learnt about Sequential Color with Memory
I learnt that SECAMM uses 625 lines per screen at 25 frames per second

What I learnt about Chroma-key
I learnt that chroma-key is when a color is replaced by an image (green or blue screen)

What I learnt about sound
I learnt that there are two characterstics of sound - amplitude and frequency

What I learnt about frequency
I learnt that frequency is measured in hertz where 1 hertz is 1 cycle per second

What I learnt about digital audio
I learnt that through digital audio, sound is stored as binary (1 or 0)

What I learnt about raw data sizes of audio files
I learnt that using Length x Sampling Rate x Bit depth x Channels equals the raw data size

What I learnt about the difference between MP3 and MP4
I also learnt that MP3 is not derived from any other MP however MP4 comes from MP2
