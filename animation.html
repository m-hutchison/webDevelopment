Multimedia Animation




















Things that I have learnt about animation in Multimedia:

What I learnt about animation
I learnt that animation is possible due to persistence of vision

What I learnt about 2D animation
I learnt that in 2D animations there are flat changes, like colors

What I learnt about 2.5D
I learnt that in 2.5D animation is using depths to make an illusion such as a drop shadow

What I learnt about cel animation
I learnt that cel animation uses plastic sheets to create the animation

What I learnt about kinematics
I learnt that kinematics involves studying movements that have joints

What I learnt about inverse kinematics
I learnt that inverse kinematic equations can determine positions of joins in a model

What I learnt about forward kinematics
I learnt that forward kinematics is used for games and robots which can create poses

What I learnt about keyframes
I learnt that key frames are the first and last frame of actions

What I learnt about rotoscoping
I learnt that rotoscoping is taking a movie and turning it into an image file (gif)

What I learnt about the advantages of animation
I learnt that animation is great for attracting attention and can have a major impact if used correctly
